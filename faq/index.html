FAQ

  1. How to register?

    The event is open to everyone, you can register for here.

  2. Will there be hands-on workshops?

    Yes, there will be. We will use Jupiter Notebooks containing examples on the Qiskit library. You can find the materials here.

  3. What is the expected level of quantum computing knowledge for attendees?

    No prior knowledge of QC is required, but surely already having basics of Linear Algebra and Quantum Mechanics makes it easy to understand the topics.

  4. Can I participate remotely or watch recorded sessions if I can't attend in person?

    Sure, the event will also be broadcast.

  5. What should I bring or prepare before attending the event?

    Your personal computer and a lot of curiosity.

  6. Is there a Q&A session or a way to ask questions during the event?
